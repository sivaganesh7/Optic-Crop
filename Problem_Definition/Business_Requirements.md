# Business Requirements

## Business Objective

Develop an intelligent crop recommendation system that assists farmers in selecting the most suitable crop based on soil and climatic conditions.

## Functional Requirements

- User can enter soil nutrient values.
- User can enter environmental conditions.
- System predicts the best crop.
- Display prediction confidence.
- Store prediction history.
- Generate recommendation reports.

## Non-Functional Requirements

- User-friendly interface.
- Fast prediction response.
- High prediction accuracy.
- Secure user data.
- Scalable architecture.

## Stakeholders

- Farmers
- Agricultural Researchers
- Government Agencies
- Agricultural Consultants
- Policy Makers

## Expected Outcomes

- Improved agricultural productivity.
- Reduced farming risks.
- Efficient resource utilization.
- Scientific decision-making.
