# Epic 1: Problem Definition and Understanding

## Overview

This epic focuses on understanding the agricultural problem, gathering business requirements, studying existing research, and analyzing the impact of implementing an AI-powered crop recommendation system.

## Objectives

- Define the agricultural problem.
- Gather business and user requirements.
- Study existing crop recommendation techniques.
- Analyze social and business impact.

## Stories

- Problem Statement
- Business Requirements
- Literature Survey
- Social and Business Impact

## Deliverables

- Problem Statement Document
- Business Requirements Specification
- Literature Survey Report
- Social and Business Impact Analysis

##  Success Criteria
Clear understanding of the agricultural problem.
Well-documented business and user requirements.
Comprehensive review of existing research and solutions.
Identification of project scope, limitations, and risks.
Stakeholder needs are documented and validated.
## Expected Outcomes

At the end of this epic, the project team will have a clear understanding of the problem domain, user requirements, existing research, business objectives, and the expected impact of the AI-powered crop recommendation system. This foundation will guide the design and development phases of the project.
