# Social and Business Impact

## Social Impact

OptiCrop supports sustainable agriculture by helping farmers make informed crop selection decisions.

### Benefits

- Higher crop yields.
- Reduced farming losses.
- Better use of fertilizers.
- Efficient water management.
- Increased farmer awareness.

## Business Impact

The system can benefit agricultural organizations and government agencies by providing data-driven insights.

### Benefits

- Improved agricultural planning.
- Better resource allocation.
- Increased productivity.
- Reduced operational costs.
- Enhanced decision-making.

## Environmental Impact

- Reduced fertilizer wastage.
- Efficient water usage.
- Improved soil health.
- Sustainable farming practices.

## Future Scope

- Fertilizer recommendation.
- Crop disease detection.
- Yield prediction.
- Weather forecasting integration.
- Mobile application support.
